The Detache Technique

The detache is the simplest of all the bowing techniques. It is simply the down-bow and up-bow stroke of the bow into the string. It is also called the normal bowing as it only plays one note on each bow stroke.


It is commonly called the simple detache and one cannot call it a simple detache if one full bow stroke was not performed before the next note to be played takes over.
