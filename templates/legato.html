The Chords Technique

The legato is a variation of the detache in which two or more notes are slurred. Simple to say, several notes can be played on one bow stroke with a soft and smooth transition to each note.


Legato is commonly represented in the sheet music using the arch above the notes.

The best example here is P.I.Tschaikovsky's Violin Concerto.
